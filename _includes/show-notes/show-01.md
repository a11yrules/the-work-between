
### About Mia

Mia Seljubac is a games lawyer, game developer, and co-founder of Cereal Mug Games. She's currently building [*I Am the Cat*](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3719920/I_am_the_cat/), a cozy narrative walking simulator where all characters are hand-painted in watercolor.
