The Work Between is committed to protecting your privacy. You can visit this website without revealing who you are or any personal information about yourself.

This policy explains what limited information is collected when you visit this site or listen to the podcast, and how it is used.

## Website Analytics

This site uses [Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io), a privacy-respecting analytics tool. Plausible does not use cookies. It does not collect personally identifiable information. It does not track you across sites or devices.

The data Plausible collects is aggregated and anonymous. It is used to understand which pages and episodes are getting traffic, and to improve the site. No individual user is identified or profiled.

## Podcast Listening Analytics

The podcast feed uses [OP3](https://op3.dev) (Open Podcast Prefix Project) as an analytics service. OP3 logs anonymized download data to measure episode reach. It does not collect personally identifiable information. OP3's data and privacy practices are publicly documented at [op3.dev](https://op3.dev).

## Links to Other Sites

This site contains links to external sites not operated by The Work Between. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy statement of any site that collects personal information.

## Legal Disclosure

We reserve the right to disclose information as required by law, or when disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or comply with a court order or legal process.
